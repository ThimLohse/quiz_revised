Let's get your quiz on!

Welcome to Quiz Quest! This is a quiz game for you and your friends that will challange your knowledge but also your speed. Just login, if you are already a member, or register, if you are new to the game.

The game is simple. You will choose a quiz, wait for your friends to join the quiz, and then it's all about knowledge and speed. You will be presented with a question and four possible answers. The challange is to choose the right answer as fast as possible. The faster your are, the more points you get.

Good Luck!
